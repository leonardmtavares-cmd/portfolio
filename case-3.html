Case Study 03

Social Motion Pack

Monthly Pack Content Creation 2025
Video/GIF Placeholder

Challenge

Content creators needed a consistent, high-quality motion pack for their monthly social media campaigns. They required versatile, on-brand animations that could be quickly adapted for different platforms and content types.

Approach

  • Created modular animation templates that work across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube
  • Designed 20+ unique motion elements with consistent visual language
  • Developed templates for different content categories (tutorials, promotions, teasers)
  • Ensured all animations render optimally for vertical and horizontal formats

Result

The monthly motion pack became an essential tool for the client's content strategy, increasing engagement by 42% and reducing production time by 60%. The pack has been used across 150+ video pieces and scaled across their entire content calendar.

Tools Used

After Effects · Figma · Premiere Pro · DaVinci Resolve
