Case Study 02

App Onboarding Flow

UI Animation Product Design 2025
Video/GIF Placeholder

Challenge

The mobile app had a complex onboarding process that caused 45% of new users to drop off before completing signup. We needed to redesign the experience with smooth animations to guide users through the flow intuitively.

Approach

  • Analyzed user behavior data to identify friction points in onboarding
  • Designed micro-interactions that provide feedback at each step
  • Created transitions between screens that establish spatial relationships
  • Implemented scroll-triggered animations to maintain engagement

Result

The redesigned onboarding flow reduced drop-off rate to 18%, increased completion rate by 58%, and improved user satisfaction score from 3.2 to 4.7 stars. The animation-driven experience became a key retention driver.

Tools Used

Figma · After Effects · Rive · Framer
